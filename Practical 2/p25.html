Network

A network is a system of interconnected computers and devices that share resources and data. Networks can be local (LAN) or global (WAN), allowing devices to communicate and exchange information

Topology

Topology refers to the physical or logical layout of how devices are connected in a network.

Internet & Its Applications

The internet is a global network that connects millions of private, public, academic, and government networks.

WWW (World Wide Web)

The WWW is a system of interlinked documents and multimedia content accessible via the internet using a web browser. It consists of websites, webpages, and online applications.
